Event Description

Financial literacy is a crucial skill that everyone should have, but it can often be a dry and intimidating topic. That's why we're bringing you a session on financial literacy with a twist - we're adding a dose of comedy to make it more engaging and enjoyable! During this session, you'll learn about important financial concepts and strategies, such as budgeting, saving, investing, and managing debt. But instead of just hearing lectures and taking notes, you'll also get to enjoy some humorous skits and jokes that make the information more memorable and relatable. Whether you're a financial novice or an experienced investor, this session is sure to have something for you. So come join us for a fun and informative evening of financial literacy and comedy!
